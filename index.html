On Saturday, November 22nd, coffee shops and local business across Kansas will come together to help raise funds for the organization Support Ethiopia Reads.

About Ethiopia Reads

Ethiopia Reads is a tax-exempt 501(c)3 organization founded in 1998 with the goal of providing much needed in-language books to the children of Ethiopia. The vision quickly grew and by 2012, Ethiopia Reads had planted over 40 libraries across Ethiopia and had kicked off an unmatched librarian and educator peer mentoring and training program.

Today, Ethiopia Reads is a new organization with a focus on schools, libraries, young athlete education and mentorship and educator training. We move forward with a focus toward working within Ethiopian communities and partnering with those communities to cultivate minds for a stronger tomorrow.

To date, Ethiopia Reads has served over 130,000 children.

Mission

Ethiopia Reads collaborates with Ethiopian communities to build schools, plant libraries, teach teachers, boost literacy and provide youth and families with the tools to improve their lives.

Vision

Ethiopia Reads empowers and strengthens communities through the art of learning and the science of teaching. We believe that providing quality schools, books and learning initiatives that engage both the creative and logical mind will lead to a more resourceful generation of leaders in Ethiopia.
